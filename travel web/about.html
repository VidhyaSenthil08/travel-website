About World Travel

This agency was born out of my head over heels love for travel not only doing it myself, but witnessing the joy it brings into the lives of others. Nothing makes me happier than knowing I have helped my clients make memories that will last a lifetime. I have spent years stacking up travel qualifications, and I am proud to have quite few under my belt. As well as holding a Masters from Royal Caribbean International, I am a Travel Specialist, Carnival Cruise Specialist, Mexico Sun and Sand Specialist and Mexico Romance and Luxury Specialist, Hawaiian Destination Specialist, Disney Travel Specialist and more besides. But while my home base hasnt shifted much, I have travelled widely, undertaking several trips a year to places such as Cozumel, Haiti, Jamaica, the Bahamas, St. Martin, St. Kits, and the Cayman Islands. With years of experience of cruises in particular, I have been lucky enough to build the insider knowledge and key contacts that make World Exclusive Travel experiences so special. Because I know the difference between an ordinary travel experience and a truly unforgettable one is all in the detail, I put in the extra legwork to make sure every last element is considered. I will check in with you along the way, sending you dining recommendations for layovers, texting you your flight details, and making sure you are never anything short of delighted with your trip.
